Backend Python Engineer

About the role
We are looking for a Backend Python Engineer to join our product engineering team working on a recruitment platform used by HR teams and hiring managers. The team is building internal APIs and backend services that support candidate search, application workflows and recruiter dashboards.

Responsibilities
You will design, build and maintain REST APIs for new platform features.
You will develop backend services with Python and FastAPI.
You will model and query application data in MongoDB.
You will collaborate with frontend developers, product managers and QA to deliver reliable features.
You will improve performance, monitoring and deployment quality of backend services.

Required skills
- Python
- FastAPI
- MongoDB
- Docker
- REST API design

Bonus skills
- CI/CD experience would be a plus.
- AWS exposure would be a plus.

Profile
We are looking for someone with at least 3 years of experience in backend development. This is a mid-level role. Strong communication in English is important because the team works across multiple locations.

This full-time role is based in Tunis, Tunisia and follows a hybrid work model.
